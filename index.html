About DocNowLk

DocNowLk is Sri Lanka's leading online hospital channeling platform that connects patients with healthcare professionals through a seamless digital experience.

Our Mission

To revolutionize healthcare access in Sri Lanka by providing a convenient, efficient, and patient-centric platform for medical appointments and consultations.

Key Features

  • Online appointment booking with specialist doctors
  • Virtual consultations from anywhere
  • Real-time appointment management
  • Secure medical records storage
  • 24/7 access to healthcare services

Why Choose Us?

With DocNowLk, you get access to a network of verified healthcare professionals across Sri Lanka. Our platform is designed to save you time and provide you with the best medical care when you need it most.
